OnePlus 6T Mclaren Edition is back starting today

ARTICLE | BY JOSEPH KAMINISKI | APRIL 7, 2019 10:54 AM PDT

NEW DELHI: OnePlus 6T McLaren Edition is back on Amazon. Considered the company’s ‘most powerful’ smartphone, it will be available on the e-commerce website starting today, April 6.

For those unaware,OnePLus 6T McLaren Edition was launched in 2018 to mark OnePlus’s fifth anniversary. The phone features a special design inspired by the F1 McLaren racing team and comes with the signature McLaren colour, Papaya Orange races, around the bottom edge.

Major highlights of the special edition are Warp Charge 30 technology, Qualcomm Snapdragon 845 processor and 10GB of RAM. The Warp Charge 30 feature is claimed by the company to charge 50% of the battery in just 20 minutes.

OnePlus 6T McLaren Edition: Price

OnePlus McLaren Edition retails at Rs 50,999. It is the costliest of the total five variants of OnePlus 6T as a standard OnePlus 6T comes with a starting price of Rs 37,999.

OnePlus 6T McLaren Edition: Specifications

OnePLus 6T McLaren Edition sports a 6.41- inch FHD+ AMOLED display with Corning Gorilla Glass 6 layer on the top. The screen offers an aspect ratio of 19.5:9 and a pixel resolution of 2340x1080. As mentioned before, the device is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 845 processor and packs 10GB of RAM.

The smartphone offers a 256GB internal storage. The McLaren edition -- like the standard OnePlus 6T -- also comes with water drop notch and an in-display fingerprint sensor.

On the camera front, OnePlus 6T McLaren edition boasts of a 16-megapixel front camera with f/2.0 aperture for selfies. The dual rear camera setup at the back comprises of 16-megapixel Sony IMX519 sensor with f/1.7 aperture and a 20-megapixel Sony IMX376K sensor with f/1.7 aperture. The phone features dual LED flash as well. The device is backed by 3,700mAh battery.

Asus ROG line up launched, starts at ksh 235,000/=

ARTICLE | BY JOSEPH KAMINISKI | APRIL 7, 2019 10:54 AM PDT

Taiwanese brand Asus has unveiled the latest range of gaming laptops under the Republic of Games (ROG) series. These include ROG Zephyrus S GX531, ROG Zephyrus S GX701, and ROG Strix SCAR II along with desktop GL12CX. While the ROG Zephyrus S GX531 is priced at Rs 2,39,990, the ROG Zephyrus S GX70 comes with a price tag of Rs 3,49,990. Both the devices will be available from April onwards.

The desktop GL12CX is available at Rs 3,29,990. The ROG Strix SCAR II comes with a starting price of Rs 1,64,990. Lastly, the Asus Hero II laptop is also priced at Rs 1,64,990. All these can be purchased starting today, March 11.

This Huawei laptop aim to take on Apple's latest MacBook

ARTICLE | BY JOSEPH KAMINISKI | APRIL 7, 2019 10:54 AM PDT

Huawei at its pre-MWC 2019 conference unveiled the 2019 version of MateBook X Pro, which is targeted at jet-setting business users and professional content creators. The laptop, which has launched alongside the Huawei Mate X foldable smartphone, at pre-MWC 2019 event in Barcelona, Spain, comes as a successor to the last year’s MateBook X Pro.

Huawei’s new MateBook X Pro sports a CNC machined metallic unibody with ‘sandblasted’ finish, giving a premium look. The computing machine is available in Mystic Silver and Space Gray colour variants and is highly portable with 14.6mm thickness (max) and 4.6mm at the thinnest.

Asus ZenBook 13, 14 and 15 laptops launched at a starting price of ksh 70,000

ARTICLE | BY JOSEPH KAMINISKI | APRIL 7, 2019 10:54 AM PDT

After introducing its Zenbook 2018 line up in Taiwan back in August last year, Asus has finally brought its Zenbook 13-inch, 14-inch and 15-inch models to the Indian shelves. The firm claims these new computing machines as one of the most compact laptops in the markets right now.

While the Asus Zenbook 15 (UX533) is being touted for its ‘immersive’ 4K display, physical numeric keypad and a more lifestyle-centric design, the ZenBook 14 (UX433) and 13 (UX333) is highlighted for its compact profile, lost lasting battery backup and much more. The laptops have the iconic ‘ErgoLift’ hinge, something we have seen in other ZenBook laptops as well.

Asus unveils a refreshed range of TUF series gaming laptops, price starts Rs 79,990

ARTICLE | BY JOSEPH KAMINISKI | APRIL 7, 2019 10:54 AM PDT

Asus has expanded its TUF range of gaming laptops with FX505 and FX705 models. The FX505 and FX705 will be available across all channels, both online and offline, at a starting price of Rs 79,990 and Rs 1,24,990 respectively.

At 15.6” and 17.3” respectively, these gaming laptops draw their power from up to Intel Core i7-8750H processors and up to Nvidia GeForce GTX 1060 graphics with up to 144Hz high-refresh rate NanoEdge displays. The company claims that gamers will also appreciate the gigabit Ethernet and gigabit-class Wi-Fi and DTS surround-sound audio experience.

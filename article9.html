Asus ZenBook 13, 14 and 15 laptops launched at a starting price of ksh 70,000

ARTICLE | BY JAMES HARRIS SIMONS | APRIL 4, 2019 9:50 PM PDT

After introducing its Zenbook 2018 line up in Taiwan back in August last year, Asus has finally brought its Zenbook 13-inch, 14-inch and 15-inch models to the Indian shelves. The firm claims these new computing machines as one of the most compact laptops in the markets right now.


While the Asus Zenbook 15 (UX533) is being touted for its ‘immersive’ 4K display, physical numeric keypad and a more lifestyle-centric design, the ZenBook 14 (UX433) and 13 (UX333) is highlighted for its compact profile, lost lasting battery backup and much more. The laptops have the iconic ‘ErgoLift’ hinge, something we have seen in other ZenBook laptops as well.


Asus ZenBook price in India


While the Asus ZenBook 13 and 14 come in different variants, the ZenBook 15 will be available in just one variant.


The ZenBook 13 comes at a starting price of Ksh 105,999 and goes up to Ksh 145,999 for the maxed out version. The Ksh 106,999 ZenBook 14 has a base price of , which stretches up to Ksh 147,999 The 15-inch model just comes in one variant, priced at Ksh 204,999.


All these laptops will be made available on Computech-Electronics, Flipkart, Amazon, Paytm and offline stores starting January 30.


Asus Zenbook specifications


The latest laptop models come powered by 8th Generation Intel Core i7 quad-core CPU and can support up to NVidia GeForce GTX 1050 Max-Q graphics and 16GB RAM. Select laptops can also be configured with up to 4K UHD resolution as well. On the connectivity front, the new laptops in the series include ports such as USB 3.1 Gen 2, USB Type-C, HDMI port, microSD card reader, SD card reader and USB Type-A ports.

We are confident that our consumers will love our new Zenbook range priced competitively.”

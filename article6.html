Asus ROG line up powered by NVID RTY launched, price starts at ksh 180,000/=

ARTICLE | BY DAVID TEPPER | APRIL 5, 2019 2:30 PM PDT

Taiwanese brand Asus has unveiled the latest range of gaming laptops under the Republic of Games (ROG) series. These include ROG Zephyrus S GX531, ROG Zephyrus S GX701, and ROG Strix SCAR II along with desktop GL12CX. While the ROG Zephyrus S GX531 is priced at Ksh 350,000/= the ROG Zephyrus S GX70 comes with a price tag of Ksh 399,999/=. Both the devices will be available from April onwards.

The desktop GL12CX is available at Ksh 349,999/=. The ROG Strix SCAR II comes with a starting price of Ksh 249,000/=. Lastly, the Asus Hero II laptop is also priced at Ksh 254,999/=. All these can be purchased starting today, April 5.


Asus ROG Zephyrus S GX531


Claimed by the company to be the world’s slimmest gaming laptop, ROG Zephyrus S GX531 measures about 15.35~16.15mm and offers a 15.6-inch display. It features NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2070 Max-Q and is powered by the latest Intel Core i7-8750H CPU. It offers 144Hz refresh rate display and 3ms response time. Other features include AAS cooling system, 12v anti-dust cooling fans, 5 heat pipes and 4 heat sinks.

Asus ROG Zephyrus S GX701GX


The laptop sports a 17.3-inch anti-glare display and comes with up to 8th Gen Intel Core i7 processors and NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2080 GPU. The device offers a 144Hz 3ms response time display and boasts of thin bezel design offering an 81% screen to body ratio. The Asus ROG S GX701GX offers an exclusive GPU Switch that easily transitions between better gaming and longer battery life. Other features include AAS cooling system, per-key RGB Lighting and Aura Sync, which also extends illumination to volume roller for quick adjustment.


Asus ROG Strix SCAR II


The 15.6-inch ROG Strix SCAR II comes with 144Hz high refresh rate and 3ms response time. It is powered by NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2070 graphics paired with 8GB of VRAM. Asus ROG Strix SCAR comes equipped with the latest 8th Generation Intel Core i7-8750H six-core CPU. Its keyhighlights are HyperStrike Pro Keyboard, AURA RGB backlight zones, prominent WASD keys and 1.8mm key travel distance.


Arnold Su, ROG and PC Head of Asus India said, “ROG is more than just a gaming brand; it’s a commitment to enhancing your gaming and enthusiast experience through innovative engineering and thoughtful design. Staying true to our promise today we launch the new ROG line up powered by the new NVIDIA GeForce RTX lineup, including the GeForce RTX 2080 on the GL12CX desktop, and the GeForce RTX 2060, RTX 2070 powering the ROG GL504 laptop pairing real-time ray tracing and artificial intelligence with cutting edge gaming and VR performance. Each one offers innovative cooling to ensure that the products deliver unprecedented sustained gaming performance. We are confident with the new RTX line up the ROG segment has become an even more exciting segment to look forward.”

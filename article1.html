Samsung Galaxy A90 specifications leaked online

ARTICLE | BY JOSEPH KAMINISKI | APRIL 7, 2019 10:54 AM PDT

Samsung Galaxy A90 is expectedto be unveiled by thecompany on April 10.Two days ahead of the launch, the phone has appeared in an online leak,revealing its key specs and features

As spotted on the Chinese microblogging website Weibo, the phone will offer a 6.7-inch FHD+ OLED screen with 1080x2400 pixel resolution. It is said to be powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon SoC processor. Further, the phone will come with triple-lens camera at the back. It will consist of a 48MP primary sensor with f/2.0 aperture along with a 8MP secondary sensor with an aperture of f/2.4 and a ToF sensor with f/1 .2 aperture.

The latest leak also reveals that the device will be backed by 3700mAh battery. It may offer 25W fast charge technology support as well. It also confirms the previous rumours that the handset may feature a ‘sliding and rotating camera system’ at the front.

According to the leak, the device will measure 76.5 x 165.0 x 9.0 mm and will weigh 219 grams. The device is expected to run on Samsung One UI based on Android 9 Pie operating system.

Meanwhile, the company’s Galaxy A20 smartphone is now available for sale in India. The handset is available on Samsung e-Store, Samsung Opera House, leading e-commerce portals and retail stores across the country starting today. Samsung Galaxy A20 comes with a 6.4-inch Super AMOLED Infinity-V display and offers a battery of 4,000mAh. It packs 3GB of RAM and 32GB internal storage. It is priced at Rs 12,490 and houses a battery of 4000mAh battery. Samsung Galaxy A20 comes in three different colour variants - Red, Blue and Black.

Samsung Galaxy A20's first sale today: Price, offers and more

ARTICLE | BY RAY DALIO | APRIL 7, 2019 12:32 PM PDT

Samsung recently launched its Galaxy A20 smartphone in Kenya. The phone is all set to go on its first sale today. It will be available on Computech-Electronics, Samsung e-Store, Samsung Opera House, leading e-commerce portals and retail stores across the country.

Samsung Galaxy A20 comes with a 6.4-inch display and offers a battery of 4,000mAh. It offers Super AMOLED infinity-V display and packs 3GB of RAM.

Samsung Galaxy A20: Price

The smartphone was first launched in Indonesia.

Samsung Galaxy A20 comes in three colour options - Red, Blue and Black. It is priced at Rs 12,490 in India.

Samsung Galaxy A20: Specifications

As far as the specifications are concerned, the Samsung Galaxy A20 smartphone offers a 6.4-inch Super AMOLED display with 720x1560 pixel resolution. It is powered by Exynos 7884 Octa-core processor and packs 3GB of RAM.

The handset runs Samsung One UI based on the latest Android Pie operating system. It offers an onboard storage of 32GB which can be expanded further using the microSD card.

For imaging duties, the smartphone boasts of 8-megapixel sensor with f/2.0 aperture at the front for selfies. There is a dual lens setup at the back. It consists of 13-megapixel primary sensor with f/1.9 aperture and 5-megapixel secondary sensor with f/2.2 aperture, respectively.

Samsung Galaxy A20 is backed by 4000mAh battery. The device comes with 15W fast charging adapter and USB Type C connectivity. There is a rear mounted fingerprint sensor at the back for security. As stated above, Samsung Galaxy A20 comes in three different colour variants that include Red, Blue and Black.

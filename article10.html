Asus has expanded its TUF range of gaming laptops with FX505 and FX705 models.
| Gadgets Now | Nov 22, 2018, 06:57PM IST
Asus has expanded its TUF range of gaming laptops with FX505 and FX705 models. The FX505 and FX705 will be available across all channels, both online and offline, at a starting price of Rs 79,990 and Rs 1,24,990 respectively.

At 15.6” and 17.3” respectively, these gaming laptops draw their power from up to Intel Core i7-8750H processors and up to Nvidia GeForce GTX 1060 graphics with up to 144Hz high-refresh rate NanoEdge displays. The company claims that gamers will also appreciate the gigabit Ethernet and gigabit-class Wi-Fi and DTS surround-sound audio experience.

As per the company, both FX505 and FX705 have passed the MIL-810G military-grade tests for endurance. They feature the RGB keyboard in addition to WASD key cap design for intuitive controls. With the Overstroke technology, the keyboard, as per the company, registers keystrokes faster for precise and effortless control.


Asus TUF Desktop – FX10CP


The company also launched the FX10CP desktop, priced at Rs 91,990. It comes with GTX 1060 NVIDIA GPU, i7 Intel processor and isolated air chambers.


The desktop features i7-8700 processor, up to GTX1060 graphics card and up to 32GB DDR4 2666MHz RAM. It comes with a 128GB M.2 SSD primary storage and 1TB 7200RPM HDD secondary storage. The desktop is available in offline channels.


Speaking about the latest product launch, Arnold Su, ROG & PC Head, Asus India, said, “As one of the most trusted global tech brands, Asus has constantly endeavoured to empower its customers with innovative products which operate at the intersection of power and affordability. The launch of the latest TUF Gaming laptops is testimony to this vision. Both FX505 and FX705 have been designed to deliver unmatched performance, durability, and style, and are perfect for gamers who prefer an immersive gaming experience. We are confident that our latest offerings will meet and exceed the expectations of the Indian gaming community and will receive heartening market reception.”
